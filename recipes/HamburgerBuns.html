Belle's Hamburger Buns

Description

They are so easy to make: light and fluffy as well as beautiful to look at

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Combine the milk, 1 cup of water, butter, sugar and salt in a saucepan
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the flour and yeast. Pour in wet ingredients and stir until the dough starts to pull together.
  3. Punch down the dough and divide into 12 portions They should be a little larger than a golf ball
  4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Mix together the egg yolk and 1 tablespoon of water in a cup or small bowl.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
